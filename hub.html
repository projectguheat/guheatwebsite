GUHeat Engine


GUHeat Engine is a Google Earth Engine (GEE) App developed by Project GUHeat. The app automates the process of generating LST from Landsat Images in the Philippines. You can access the this GEE app through the link below.

GUHeat Engine



GUHeat Toolbox


This toolbox is a QGIS plugin developed by Project GUHeat. One of its tools is to process LST from downloaded Landsat Images with the use of QGIS software. You can download the toolbox through the link below.
